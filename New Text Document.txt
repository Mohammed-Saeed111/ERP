In Part 9 of our MERN Stack Inventory Management System series, we build the Admin Dashboard Overview.

This dashboard provides a complete real-time summary of the system, helping administrators monitor business performance and inventory status efficiently.

📊 Dashboard Features:

Total Products
Total Stock Available
Total Suppliers
Total Orders
Total Revenue
Low Stock Products Alert
High Selling Products
Out of Stock Products

By the end of this part, you’ll have a fully functional admin dashboard that gives clear insights into your inventory system using data from your Node.js & MongoDB backend and a responsive React frontend.
